Everyday we're exposed to more content than ever before. We read, watch and learn so many useful things, but much of what we learn is fleeting, and research has shown that we forget 90% of it within just a month. Some people take notes or bookmark what they learn, but hardly ever go back to them because they're not structured in a way that makes it easy for us to review. Nuggets of Wisdom aims to provide an effective, hassle-free solution to help users remember what they learn.

FAQ

What is a nugget?
Anything that you've learned that is worth remembering, in 200 characters or less.
Why would I want to use Nuggets of Wisdom?
Nuggets of Wisdom provides a simple way to organize the things you've learned, and helps you retain them. It forces you to condense and interpret the things you've identified as valuable information, into something that can be easily reviewed and remembered.
